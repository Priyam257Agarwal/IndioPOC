Flight Information

You can check the real-time status of your flight by entering your flight number or destination in the 'Flight Status' section on our website. We'll also notify you via email or SMS if there are any changes to your flight schedule.

Boarding begins 45 minutes before departure and closes 15 minutes prior to the scheduled take-off time. Please ensure you arrive at your gate on time to avoid any delays. For international flights, we recommend arriving at least 3 hours before departure.

We offer a variety of meal options on board, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free choices. You can select your preferred meal during the booking process or update it in the 'Manage Booking' section at least 24 hours before your flight.
